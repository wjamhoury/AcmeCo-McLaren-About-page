About McLaren

Our History

McLaren is a British racing and sports car manufacturer founded in 1963 by New Zealander Bruce McLaren. The company initially started as a racing team, and later expanded to include production of high-performance sports cars. McLaren has won numerous championships in various racing series, including Formula One.

Our Cars

McLaren produces a range of high-performance sports cars, including the McLaren 720S, McLaren 570S, and McLaren GT. All of McLaren's cars feature cutting-edge technology and exceptional performance, making them some of the most sought-after sports cars in the world.

Our Achievements

McLaren has a long and impressive racing history, with numerous championships and victories in Formula One, Can-Am, and other racing series. Some of the most notable achievements include three Formula One World Championships, 182 Grand Prix victories, and 12 Indianapolis 500 victories.

In addition to racing, McLaren has also set numerous world records with its production cars, including the McLaren F1's record-breaking top speed of 240.1 mph.
